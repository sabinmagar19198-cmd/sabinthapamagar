About Me

Engineer. Problem Solver.
Lifelong Learner.

I am Sabin Thapa Magar, an electronics engineer who completed my Bachelor's Degree in Electronics, Communication & Information Engineering from Kantipur Engineering College (KEC), Dhapakhel, Lalitpur — affiliated to Tribhuvan University — in 2025 with First Division.

My academic journey at KEC (2020–2025) built a solid foundation across digital systems, communication engineering, embedded systems, antenna design, and industrial automation.

I completed hands-on training in Raspberry Pi with IoT and an intensive Industrial Automation course covering PLC (Siemens & Delta), HMI, and SCADA. I competed in robotics events at HEX-2023, YATRA 4.0, and ICT Mela 4.0, building real hardware and working in teams under pressure.

I am passionate about bridging academic electronics and practical real-world applications — whether in embedded hardware, industrial control systems, wireless communication, or AI-assisted electronics.
