About Me

Engineer. Builder.
Lifelong Learner.

I am Sabin Thapa Magar, an electronics engineer with a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics, Communication & Information Engineering from Kantipur Engineering College (KEC), Dhapakhel, Lalitpur — affiliated to Tribhuvan University — completed in 2025 with First Division.

Born on 12 February 2003, son of Gopal Thapa Magar. My four-year academic journey at KEC built a strong foundation across digital systems, communication engineering, embedded systems, antenna design, industrial automation, and AI-integrated applications.

My minor project was an IoT-based home automation system using ESP32 and MQTT, which I further explored through a security vulnerability lens — analysing weak authentication, unencrypted MQTT channels, and device exposure risks. My major project was a real-time virtual eyewear try-on system built with VGG16, MTCNN (92% detection accuracy), Flask, MySQL, WebRTC, and WebGL — combining deep learning with live video inference.

I completed 120+ hours of self-initiated hands-on training in IoT and industrial automation, competed in three national robotics events, and am driven by the intersection of hardware security, embedded intelligence, and real-world engineering systems.
