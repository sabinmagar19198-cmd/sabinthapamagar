About Me

Engineer. Problem Solver.
Lifelong Learner.

I am Sabin Thapa Magar, an electronics engineer who completed my Bachelor's Degree in Electronics, Communication & Information Engineering from Kantipur Engineering College (KEC), Dhapakhel, Lalitpur - affiliated to Tribhuvan University - in the year 2025 with First Division.

Son of Gopal Thapa Magar, I was born on 12 February 2003. My academic journey at KEC spanned from 2020 to 2025, where I built a solid foundation across a wide range of engineering disciplines including digital systems, communication engineering, embedded systems, antenna design, and industrial automation.

During my degree, I participated in hands-on training in Raspberry Pi with IoT and completed an intensive course on Industrial Automation covering PLC (Siemens & Delta), HMI, and SCADA systems. I competed in robotics events at HEX-2023, YATRA 4.0, and ICT Mela 4.0, building real engineering hardware and working in teams under pressure.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between academic electronics and practical, real-world applications - whether that is in embedded hardware, industrial control systems, wireless communication, or emerging technologies like AI-assisted electronics.
