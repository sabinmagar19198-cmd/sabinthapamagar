Research Publication

Peer-Reviewed Work

Research published in an international engineering journal — extending the findings of my final year major project into the academic domain.

International Journal on Engineering Technology (INJET)  ·  Vol. 2, No. 2  ·  May 2025  ·  NepJOL / Tribhuvan University
A Web-Based AR-Powered Virtual Eyewear Try-On System
Prakriti Thapa  ·  Pratap Niraula  ·  Sabin Thapa Magar  ·  Sunil Bahadur Singh  ·  Sachin Shrestha
DOI: 10.3126/injet.v2i2.78599  ·  Pages: 114–125  ·  Keywords: Augmented Reality  ·  Computer Vision  ·  Face Shape Classification  ·  MTCNN  ·  MediaPipe  ·  Three.js  ·  License: CC BY 4.0
This research presents a web-based virtual try-on system for eyewear that enhances the online shopping experience through computer vision and augmented reality. The platform allows users to analyze their face shape and receive personalized eyewear recommendations, with the ability to try on virtual glasses in real time. The system employs MTCNN for accurate facial region detection, VGG-16 for face shape classification across five categories (oval, round, square, heart-shaped, oblong), and MediaPipe for facial landmark localization and pupil distance calculation. Three.js provides immersive 3D visualization for realistic overlay. The system achieves a face shape classification accuracy of 92%, an average AR overlay IoU of 81%, and a width error margin of approximately 5%.
