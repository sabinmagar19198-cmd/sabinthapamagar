About

Engineer first, builder always.

My strongest work happens where electronics, software, communication systems, and practical constraints meet.

I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics, Communication and Information Engineering from Kantipur Engineering College under Tribhuvan University. The degree gave me a broad technical foundation across digital systems, embedded systems, signal processing, computer networks, RF and microwave engineering, telecommunications, AI, and information systems.

What I enjoy most is building systems that leave the classroom: sensor networks that collect real data, robots that react to physical environments, PLC workflows that match industrial processes, and connected devices that need to be reliable and secure.

My current direction is embedded security and connected hardware. I want to understand how devices are built, how they fail, and how they can be made safer in the field.
